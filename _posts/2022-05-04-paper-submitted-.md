Hey guys, I have not posted for a while because I have been full of work.
With the PerCeIVe Lab of Catania we submitted an article to IEEE Transaction on Medical Imaging for the Special Issue on Federated Learning. 
I contributed to the article creating the baselines with FedAvg and FedCurv on some datasets provided by the Catania's Lab in order to compare their proposed method (which uses GANs to synthetise privacy-preserving data) with the state of the art.
The paper is titled "Decentralized Distributed Learning with Privacy-Preserving Data Synthesis".

Now I am doing the second homework for Multivariate Statistical Analysis and continue to work on a benchmark/comparison with FedAvg vs FedCurv on 5 Non-Iidness, using several datasets such as MNIST, CIFAR10, MEDMNIST, TinyImageNet.
