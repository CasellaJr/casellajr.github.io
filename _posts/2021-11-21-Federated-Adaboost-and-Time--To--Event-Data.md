This post discusses the projects on which I started to work during this week.

I am reading recents papers about Federated Learning on non-IID Data Distributions, one of the main research area of FL. 
The idea is to discover some datasets and split them in a non-IID way and then test these data on _Federated Adaboost_, a Federated version of Adaboost developed by some researchers of UniTO.

I am also studying the theory about Time-To-Event data because our research group, _Alpha_, is collaborating with a research group of Medical Sciences, for doing Federated Learning on Time-To-Event medical data.

No news about the OpenFL framework. I am attending a fixed version in which 1 collaborator scenario is equal to a non federated case.
